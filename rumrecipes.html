Rum Cocktail Recipes

The beakers show the strength of the cocktail! Each beaker is roughly 25ml of alcohol! Enjoy!

daiquiri

Shake 50ml of Rum with 35ml of fresh lime juice and some sugar syrup.
Serve up in a martini or margarita glass and garnish with lime.

dark and stormy

Put 50ml dark rum into a Collins or Highball glass with fresh lime juice, Angostura bitters, sugar syrup and a nice fiery ginger beer!
Garnish with lime.

Piña Colada

Blend 35ml Gold rum and 10ml Coconut Rum with some pineapple juice and fresh pineapple chunks until smooth. Serve in a glass of your choice, garnished with cherry.
