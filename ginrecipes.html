Gin Cocktail Recipes

The beakers show the strength of the cocktail! Each beaker is roughly 25ml of alcohol! Enjoy!

bramble

Shake 50ml Gin, fresh lemon juice and sugar syrup well.

Pour over ice and trickle 15ml Crème de Mure, garnish with fresh blackberries!

gin-gin mule

Muddle mint leaves and sugar syrup in a shaker, add in 50ml Gin and ice and shake. Add in Ginger Beer and Lime Juice and give it a stir. Use some mint leaves and fresh lime slices to garnish.

aviation

Shake together 50ml Gin, 10ml Crème de Violette, 15ml Maraschino liquer and some fresh lemon juice. Pour into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with cherry, candied violet or lemon zest.
