appletini
50ml Vodka, 50ml Apple Juice, 1 tsp Lemon Juice and 1 tsp Maple Syrup. Shaken (not stirred) and strained into a Martini glass. Garnish with apple.

moscow mule
Add some crushed ice to 50ml vodka and top up with Ginger Beer. Stir & add bitters, a slice of lime and some mint to garnish

cosmopolitan
Shake 50ml Mandarin Vodka with some Orange Liquer, Lime Juice. 50ml cranberry juice and ice. Strain into a Martini Glass and garnish with orange zest.
